Welcome to WMS Product!

Thank you for choosing our Warehouse Management System (WMS) product. This guide will help you get started with the initial setup and basic usage of our system.

1. Setting Up Your Account

To get started, you will need to create an account. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button.
  2. Fill in your details, including your name, email address, and password.
  3. Check your email for a confirmation link and click on it to verify your account.
  4. Log in to your account using your email and password.

2. Configuring Your Warehouse

After setting up your account, you can configure your warehouse settings:

  1. Navigate to the "Settings" section in the main menu.
  2. Enter your warehouse details, including location, size, and layout.
  3. Set up different zones and sections within your warehouse for better organization.
  4. Save your settings to apply the changes.

3. Adding Inventory

Once your warehouse is configured, you can start adding inventory:

  1. Go to the "Inventory" section in the main menu.
  2. Click on the "Add Item" button.
  3. Fill in the item details, such as name, SKU, quantity, and location within the warehouse.
  4. Save the item to add it to your inventory.

4. Managing Orders

Our WMS product makes it easy to manage orders:

  1. Navigate to the "Orders" section in the main menu.
  2. Click on the "Create Order" button.
  3. Enter the order details, including customer information, items, and quantities.
  4. Save the order to process it.
  5. Track the order status and fulfillment progress within the "Orders" section.

5. Generating Reports

Gain insights into your warehouse operations by generating reports:

  1. Go to the "Reports" section in the main menu.
  2. Select the type of report you want to generate, such as inventory levels, order history, or performance metrics.
  3. Customize the report parameters to focus on specific data.
  4. Click on the "Generate Report" button to view and download the report.
